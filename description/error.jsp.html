This is sort of my catch-all error page. Anytime something goes wrong or is out of whack, you are routed here where you can hit "Return" to be taken to the home page (if logged in) or the login page (if logged out). I wanted to implement an "error message" feature, but never got around to it because I kept pushing it off and didn't want to go back and update all my code, however I could foresee that being a good feature for informing the user why they encountered an error. Also, if your session expires you are taken to this screen. Simply hit return and re-log in to get a new session and everything is back to normal.
