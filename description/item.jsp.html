This view is for giving a more detailed look at a specific item. The user can get here by clicking the item in the store, clicking random item, or clicking on the next or previous buttons on a different item page. In the center of the page is the title of the item, and a window containing a picture of the item and its description, price, quantity, and an add to cart button as well as a view cart button. On the left and right are arrows indicating the next and previous buttons. If there is no next or previous, these arrows grey out (respectively).
